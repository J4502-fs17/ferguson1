Activists in Ferguson

Mission Statement

We are trying to tell the personal stories of activists in Ferguson to reshape the narrative and tell a nationally-recognized story through a local lense.

About

After the shooting of high school graduate Michael Brown, thousands of activists gathered in the streets of Ferguson, Missouri to protest police brutality. As demonstrations increased and tensions escalated, different stories spread quickly throughout the nation about the events that occurred in Ferguson.The activists involved have their own personal experiences of Ferguson. Meet the activists, hear their stories.
