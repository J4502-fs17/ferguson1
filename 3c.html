St. Louis County and City Black Populatio

As indicated by Figure 3, illustrating the St. Louis City and County residential Black population, Black St. Louisans are concentrated within north St. Louis County and on the eastern border of the city. The historical context of Ferguson also is important to note here. Restrictive covenants created in St. Louis during the early twentieth century began unraveling as black residents challenged local land ordinances by buying real estate in the county.
