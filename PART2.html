Part Two: What Happened after Mike Brown Shooting


Shortly after this shooting took place in Ferguson, Missouri (a municipality of St. Louis County), a post on Twitter reported from Ferguson: “@stlcountypd incited a riot by bringing dogs to the face of a grieving community. Who’s the victim?”.


For many activists, the disposability of brown and black bodies was epitomized through the shooting, killing and posthumous treatment of Brown—and later through police officers’ abusive practices toward activists.
